## üöÄ Getting Started

NetworkCRM is designed to help students and professionals manage their networking efforts for investment banking, consulting, and other competitive industries. Here's how to get the most out of it.

### Creating Your Account
1. Click "Get Started" on the homepage
2. Enter your name, email, and create a password
3. You'll be taken directly to your dashboard

### Understanding the Dashboard
Your dashboard gives you a bird's-eye view of your networking progress:
- **Pipeline Overview:** See contacts at each stage (No Action ‚Üí Reached Out ‚Üí In Communication ‚Üí Referral Received)
- **Recent Activity:** Track your latest emails, meetings, and contact updates
- **Follow-up Reminders:** Never miss a follow-up with automatic tracking
- **Analytics:** Visualize your outreach efforts and response rates

## üë• Managing Contacts

Contacts are the heart of NetworkCRM. Here's how to build and manage your network effectively.

### Adding Contacts
1. Navigate to **Process ‚Üí Contacts** in the sidebar
2. Click **+ Add Contact**
3. Fill in the details:
   - **Name & Email:** Required fields
   - **Company & Title:** Who they work for and their role
   - **Department & Group:** Categorize by team (e.g., IBD, S&T)
   - **Stage:** Where they are in your pipeline
   - **Tags:** Add custom labels for filtering

### Contact Stages
Track where each contact is in your networking journey:
- **No Action:** Contact added but not yet reached out
- **Reached Out:** Initial email sent, waiting for response
- **In Communication:** Active back-and-forth conversation
- **Referral Received:** They've agreed to refer you or provide a referral

### Filtering & Search
Use the filter bar to quickly find contacts:
- Search by name or email
- Filter by company, title, department, or group
- Filter by stage to see your pipeline
- Filter by tags for custom categories

### Bulk Actions
Need to update multiple contacts? Use **Edit Multiple** to:
- Change stage for multiple contacts
- Add or remove tags in bulk
- Update company or department info

## üìß Email Integration

Connect your email to automatically sync communications with your contacts.

### Connecting Gmail
1. Go to **Settings** (gear icon in sidebar)
2. Find **Gmail Integration** section
3. Click **Connect Gmail**
4. Authorize NetworkCRM to read your emails
5. Emails with your contacts will sync automatically

### Email Import
The **Outreach ‚Üí Email Import** section shows:
- All synced emails from connected accounts
- Priority emails (unread from contacts you're tracking)
- Auto-matched contacts based on email addresses

### Watched Domains
Set up domain watching to auto-import emails from specific companies:
1. Go to **Settings ‚Üí Email**
2. Add domains (e.g., "goldmansachs.com", "jpmorgan.com")
3. Emails from these domains will be highlighted as priority

## üì§ Email Campaigns

Send personalized mass emails to multiple contacts efficiently.

### Creating Templates
1. Go to **Outreach ‚Üí Templates**
2. Click **+ New Template**
3. Write your email with merge fields:
   - {{first_name}} - Contact's first name
   - {{last_name}} - Contact's last name
   - {{company}} - Their company
   - {{title}} - Their job title
4. Choose template type: **New Email** or **Reply** (for follow-ups)

### Running a Campaign
1. Go to **Outreach ‚Üí Campaigns**
2. Click **+ New Campaign**
3. Name your campaign (e.g., "Q1 Goldman Outreach")
4. Select thread mode:
   - **New Email:** Start fresh conversations
   - **Reply to Thread:** Follow up on existing threads
5. Choose your template
6. Filter recipients by stage, company, or tags
7. Preview each email before sending
8. Send all at once or one-by-one

### Follow-Up Campaigns
For contacts who haven't responded:
1. Create a "Reply" type template
2. Create campaign with **Reply to Thread** mode
3. Filter by stage "Reached Out" to target non-responders
4. The system will reply to existing threads automatically

## üè¢ Companies & Applications

Track the firms you're targeting and your application progress.

### Managing Companies
Go to **Companies ‚Üí Companies** to:
- Add firms you're targeting
- Track company details (industry, size, location)
- See all contacts at each company
- Add notes about the firm

### Tracking Interviews
Log interviews in **Companies ‚Üí Interviews**:
- Record interview date and type (phone, video, in-person)
- Note which round (first round, superday, etc.)
- Add interviewer names and questions asked
- Track outcomes and next steps

### Application Tracker
Use **Companies ‚Üí Applications** to:
- Log every application submitted
- Track status (Applied, Interview, Offer, Rejected)
- Set deadline reminders
- Link to related contacts and interviews

## üìÖ Calendar & Meetings

Keep track of all your networking calls and coffee chats.

### Logging Meetings
1. Go to **Process ‚Üí Meetings**
2. Click **+ Add Meeting**
3. Select the contact(s) involved
4. Set date, time, and duration
5. Choose meeting type (call, video, in-person)
6. Add notes and talking points

### Calendar View
The **Calendar** page shows:
- All scheduled meetings in month/week/day view
- Upcoming interview dates
- Application deadlines
- Follow-up reminders

### After a Meeting
Best practices after each networking call:
1. Update meeting notes with key takeaways
2. Update contact stage if appropriate
3. Set follow-up reminder
4. Send thank-you email (use a template!)

## üí° Pro Tips

### Effective Tagging Strategy
- Use tags like "Alumni", "Warm Intro", "Cold Outreach"
- Tag by recruiting cycle: "FT 2026", "SA 2025"
- Tag by priority: "High Priority", "Backup"

### Follow-Up Timing
- Set follow-up reminders in Settings (default: 2 weeks)
- The dashboard shows contacts needing follow-up
- Use the "Reached Out" filter to find non-responders

### Campaign Best Practices
- Preview every email before sending
- Personalize beyond just merge fields when possible
- Send campaigns in batches (10-20 at a time)
- Wait 1-2 weeks before follow-up campaigns

### Data Organization
- Use Import/Export for bulk contact uploads
- Keep company and department fields consistent
- Regularly clean up duplicate contacts
- Archive old contacts rather than deleting
